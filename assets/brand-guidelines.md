## 1. Brand Story & Positioning

### Who We Are

DANKOS is a premium cannabis wellness brand for people who value quality, transparency, and design. We exist at the intersection of botanical science and modern luxury — where nature meets intention.

### What We Believe

- **Intention over impulse.** Every product is crafted with purpose, tested for purity, and designed for the discerning consumer.
- **Nature, refined.** We honour the plant but reject the stereotype. Premium ingredients, transparent sourcing, modern expression.
- **Wellness first.** Cannabis as a wellness tool — not a party prop.

### Brand Positioning

> *"The Apple of cannabis."* — clean, premium, and confident without saying a word.

| Dimension | Position |
|---|---|
| Price tier | Premium / ultra-premium |
| Target audience | 28–45, wellness-focused, design-conscious, culturally literate |
| Competitive frame | Premium spirits (Apothékary, Stash, Fleur) + wellness brands (Oura, Headspace) |
| Brand personality | Grounded · Confident · Refined · Approachable |

## 2. Logo Usage

### Primary Logo Lockups

1. **Wordmark** — "DANKOS" in the primary typeface. Use for hero areas, header lockup, and packaging front-of-pack.
2. **Icon + Wordmark** — Geometric leaf icon above or beside the wordmark. Use when both elements are required.
3. **Icon Standalone** — The geometric leaf mark used alone for favicons, social avatars, and small-format applications.

### Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to **½ the height of the "D"** in the wordmark. No other elements should intrude into this zone.

```
    ┌──────────────────────────────┐
    │          clear space          │
    │  ╔══════════════════════════╗ │
    │  ║       DANKOS wordmark    ║ │
    │  ╚══════════════════════════╝ │
    │          clear space          │
    └──────────────────────────────┘
```

### Minimum Size

| Format | Minimum width |
|---|---|
| Print | 30 mm (1.2 in) |
| Digital | 120 px width |
| Favicon / icon | 32 × 32 px |

### Incorrect Usage

- ✗ Do not stretch, skew, or distort the logo.
- ✗ Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or glow effects.
- ✗ Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
- ✗ Do not change the logo colours (except approved monochrome variants).
- ✗ Do not rotate the logo more than ±5° from horizontal.
- ✗ Do not add secondary graphics inside or overlapping the clear space.

### Monochrome Variants

- **White on dark:** Use the logo in pure white (`#FFFFFF`) on backgrounds darker than `#1B5E20`.
- **Single-colour:** For very small applications, reduce to a single solid colour (forest green preferred).

- Maximum line length: 65 characters for body, 45 for display.

## 5. Photography Style

### Visual Principles

1. **Moody, natural light.** Prefer late-afternoon golden hour, soft diffused windows, or directional side-lighting.
2. **Product-centric.** The product is always the hero — but it lives in a world, not on a void.
3. **Nature textures.** Raw wood, stone, linen, moss, water droplets — tactile, earthy, organic.
4. **Negative space.** Generous empty areas for breathing room. Nothing crowded.
5. **Colour palette adherence.** Shoot to complement the brand palette — avoid clashing warm or saturated tones that fight the green/gold scheme.

### Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ✗ Don't |
|---|---|
| Shoot product on natural surfaces | Place products on plain white unless needed for e-commerce |
| Use shallow depth of field for hero shots | Over-stylize or over-retouch models |
| Include natural elements (leaves, water, stone) | Use cliché cannabis imagery (marijuana leaves, ash trays, bong water) |
| Warm, earthy skin tones | Neon, club, or party aesthetics |
| Minimal composition with strong negative space | Cluttered, busy scenes with competing elements |

### Photography Categories

| Category | Direction |
|---|---|
| **Product hero** | Tight, moody, natural light, shadow play |
| **Lifestyle** | Person-in-context, wellness ritual, natural settings |
| **Texture / detail** | Macro of ingredients, packaging materials, surface details |
| **Flat lay** | Organised, geometric, palette-aligned, generous spacing |

## 6. Tone of Voice

### Voice Characteristics

| Attribute | What it means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Premium** | Confident, not boastful | "Crafted for the considered consumer." |
| **Grounded** | Rooted in nature and fact | "Botanically sourced. Third-party tested." |
| **Professional** | Clear, precise, no jargon | "Each serving contains 10 mg of full-spectrum CBD." |
| **Approachable** | Welcoming, never condescending | "Wellness, reimagined — and a little bit delicious." |

### Tone Spectrum

```
Casual ◄────────────●────────────► Authoritative
   "Hey, check this out"     "Clinical, technical"
   ← We sit slightly right of centre →
```

### Style Rules

- **No slang.** Avoid "stoned," "high," "weed," "bud," or "dope."
- **Use botanical language.** "Cultivated," "extract," "botanical," "wellness."
- **Be direct.** Short sentences. Active voice. Period.
- **Embrace subtlety.** Let the quality speak. Adjectives sparingly.
- **Never preach.** Inform, don't moralise about use.

### Copy Examples

| Context | ✓ Right | ✗ Wrong |
|---|---|---|
| Tagline | "Botanical. Intentional. DANKOS." | "The only weed brand you'll ever need!" |
| Product desc | "Full-spectrum botanical extract. 10 mg per serving." | "Super duper strong weed buds!" |
| CTA | "Explore the collection" | "Click here to get lit 🔥" |
| Wellness | "Designed for your daily ritual." | "Get high, feel good." |

## 7. Icon Style

### Principles

- **Geometric.** All icons built on a consistent grid (24 × 24 px default).
- **Stroke-based.** 2 px stroke weight for consistency. Use fills sparingly.
- **Minimal detail.** If it can be drawn in fewer strokes, it should be.
- **Consistent corner radius.** Icons use matching corner radii to the design system.

### Icon Categories

| Category | Style | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **UI** | Outline, 2 px stroke | Settings, search, cart, user |
| **Product** | Filled or outline, geometric | Leaf, droplet, flame, clock |
| **Marketing** | Geometric accent shapes | Stars, badges, ribbons |

## 8. Motion & Animation

### Principles

1. **Subtle.** No bounce, no overshoot, no dramatic transitions.
2. **Purposeful.** Every animation should aid comprehension or confirm action.
3. **Smooth.** Use easing curves, not linear.
4. **Consistent.** One set of timings across every touchpoint.

### Timing

| Action | Duration | Easing |
|---|---|---|
| Page transition | 300 ms | `cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1)` |
| Hover state | 200 ms | `ease-out` |
| Modal open/close | 250 ms | `cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)` |
| Scroll-triggered reveal | 400 ms | `cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1)` |
| Toast / notification | 3000 ms visible + 300 ms fade | Linear visible, ease-out hide |

### Animation Guidelines

- **Micro-interactions** (button press, checkbox): 150–200 ms, ease-out.
- **Scroll reveals** (section-in): 400 ms, stagger children by 50 ms.
- **Page transitions**: 300 ms cross-fade or horizontal slide.
- **Loading states**: Skeleton shimmer > spinner. If using a spinner, keep it to a rotating stroke with the brand emerald colour.
- **Motion on reduced motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Fade-only fallback, no transforms.

### Forbidden

- ✗ Elastic/bouncy easing
- ✗ Rotations greater than 360°
- ✗ Scale transforms below 0.95
- ✗ Auto-playing animations on load
- ✗ Particle effects or decorative noise
